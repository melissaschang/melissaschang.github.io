Cooking Papa

Cooking Papa creates an exciting gaming experience for players by bringing their favorite cooking game into the third dimension. Players utilize a Raspberry Pi and IMU combination to perform every required action of a recipe, and a laptop camera keeps track of their location while they traverse from one station to another. A simple trial version of the game, where the user follows a simple recipe for vegetable soup, has been created over the course of the quarter. Speech processing, IMU gesture recognition, vision processing, graphics, and MQTT communication have all been successfully implemented and integrated into Cooking Papa 2.0. Speech processing allows users to grab the necessary cooking utensil once they reach a certain appliance, while a combination of an IMU and Raspberry Pi classify and score the quality of an action. Vision processing is able to map the horizontal movements of the user to the cartoon chef. A friendly graphical interface welcomes players into the game, and MQTT allows for communication between Raspberry Pi and CPU
